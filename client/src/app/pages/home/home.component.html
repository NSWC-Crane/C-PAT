Welcome

C-PAT™ is an API and web client being developed through NAVSEA and NSWC Crane that aims to automate vulnerability documentation and tracking.

C-PAT™ is architected with an emphasis towards interoperability with other RMF Tools, and is designed to integrate with STIG Manager and Tenable.sc (ACAS) to provide a comprehensive solution throughout the RMF process.
